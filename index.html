About Me

"Your Shopify store should sell, not just exist."

I’m Waqas Ahmad, a Shopify developer who combines clean, modern design with real e-commerce business experience. From setup to launch—and beyond—I’ll help you build a store that not only looks great but is tailored to your brand, audience, and sales goals.

I can help you with:

  • Setting up a brand-new Shopify store
  • Customizing themes to match your brand
  • Fixing layout bugs or mobile responsiveness issues
  • Uploading and organizing your products
  • Connecting your domain, setting up checkout, and configuring payment gateways
  • Making basic code edits with Liquid, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript

Beyond my technical skills, I bring valuable e-commerce business experience. I know that a successful store doesn’t come from design skills alone—it comes from clear communication between the client and the freelancer, and from aligning the store with the business’s unique needs. I actively listen to my clients to fully understand their goals, and I use my business insight to guide them on strategic improvements that enhance both functionality and aesthetics. You’ll not only get a well-built Shopify store, but also extra guidance on how to make it more appealing, user-friendly, and aligned with your target audience.

Project Details: Velasca

For Velasca, an Italian footwear brand, I developed a Shopify store that marries the elegance of artisanal craftsmanship with modern e-commerce standards. I worked closely with the client to understand their brand heritage and target audience, ensuring the design reflected both tradition and contemporary style. The store features a clean, minimalist layout, high-resolution lifestyle photography, and an intuitive navigation structure to guide customers seamlessly from browsing to checkout. I also advised on visual storytelling placement, ensuring the brand’s origin story connected with visitors on an emotional level while optimizing the buying journey for higher conversions.

Project Details: Culture Hijab

For Culture Hijab, a brand serving a niche yet style-conscious audience, I created a Shopify store that balances modern design with cultural sensitivity. Through active client communication, I understood their need to present products in a way that resonates with modest fashion buyers while still appealing to a global audience. I implemented advanced product filters, mobile-optimized pages, and rich, color-accurate imagery to make browsing effortless. Beyond development, I guided the client on incorporating customer reviews, size charts, and curated style collections—enhancements that not only improved the shopping experience but also boosted trust and repeat purchases.

Project Details: Volant

For Volant, a Scandinavian diffuser and home fragrance company, I designed a Shopify store that reflects the brand’s calming and minimalist identity. Working closely with the brand, I identified the need for a layout that allowed their products to speak for themselves while still guiding users toward purchase. I used soft color palettes, refined typography, and smooth animations to create a serene browsing experience. Additionally, I advised on strategic content placement, integrating product storytelling, trust signals, and lifestyle photography that aligned with their brand ethos—helping customers connect emotionally while enjoying a frictionless shopping journey.
